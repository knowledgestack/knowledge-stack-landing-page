# Getting Started with Knowledge Stack: A Complete Guide

Welcome to Knowledge Stack! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started with our platform.

## Step 1: Sign Up

First, create your account at [app.knowledgestack.ai](https://app.knowledgestack.ai). You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

## Step 2: Create Your First Knowledge Base

Once you're logged in, you can create your first knowledge base:

1. Click "New Knowledge Base" in the dashboard
2. Give it a name (e.g., "Company Documentation")
3. Choose your data source (upload files, connect to a database, or integrate with cloud storage)

## Step 3: Upload Your Documents

Knowledge Stack supports a wide variety of file formats:

- **Documents**: PDF, Word, Markdown, Text files
- **Spreadsheets**: Excel, CSV
- **Presentations**: PowerPoint
- **Code**: GitHub repositories, code files

Simply drag and drop your files or use the upload button.

## Step 4: Start Querying

Once your documents are processed, you can start asking questions:

- "What is our refund policy?"
- "How do I reset my password?"
- "What are the key features of our product?"

Each answer includes source citations so you can verify the information.
